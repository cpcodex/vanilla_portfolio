About Me

My journey into tech wasn't a straight line. It was a path forged through passion, necessity, and a decade of hands-on problem-solving skills as an ASE Certified Mechanic. I am married to my high school sweetheart and a father of three, my world is shaped by my family, a love for technology, and a desire to build new things. Since I was a child, I was obsessed with Legos and taking things apart to know how they work; never able to put it back together. But I learned and it taught me my strongest traits is building and problem-solving via tackling something head on. A pivotal part of my life is advocating for one of my children, who has autism, which has taught me more about patience, perspective, and purpose than any classroom could.

I completed 1 1/2 years of a computer science degree at SNHU, maintaining a 3.88 GPA and Presidents list 2 different semesters. Throughout my time, I realized my true passion was in the practical application of coding and life. I made the decision to focus on self-directed learning, as I have always been a autodidact, even launching a paracord business to support my goal recently. This path has been a testament to my belief in a focused plan, proving that dedication and a clear vision are the most powerful tools for achieving any goal.

Objective

My objective is to empower small businesses by providing accessible, cutting-edge technological solutions on both the web and mobile devices. I am passionate about leveling the playing field, allowing smaller companies to compete with established enterprises by developing responsive web applications, creating intuitive mobile experiences, and integrating AI to streamline their systems. I bring a unique blend of problem-solving, relentless drive, and technical skill to help businesses grow and thrive in the modern digital landscape.
