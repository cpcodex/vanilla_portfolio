About Me

Thank you for visiting my page! My name is Nick, I spent over a decade as an ASE Certified Mechanic before I transitioned to pursue my lifelong passion in Tech. I gained many skills, hands-on problem-solving being amongst the most important to my character. This experience taught me to tackle challenges head-on with a 'can do' attitude, managing project progression with ease.

As an avid learner and autodidact, I maintained a 3.88 GPA in my Computer Science studies at SNHU. However, my true passion lies in the practical application of Code so I left my studies to pursue a more hands-on learning approach. Driven by the same curiosity that led me to take things apart as a child, this trait has led me to get after the skills I need to accomplish my goals at hand. My professional experience, as well as advocating for my child with autism, has taught me invaluable lessons in patience and purpose no classroom could, which I bring those values to every project I work on.

Objective

My objective is to build things I enjoy and find purposeful. I want to empower small businesses in my area by providing accessible, cutting-edge technological solutions on both the Web and Mobile Devices. I am passionate about leveling the playing field, allowing small businesses in my area to compete with established enterprises.

Accomplishing these goals by developing responsive modern web applications, creating intuitive mobile applications, prioritizing user experience, SEO, and integrating AI to streamline systems where necessary. I bring a unique blend of problem-solving, relentless drive, and technical skill to help businesses grow and thrive in the modern digital landscape.
