About Me

My path to tech wasn't linear; it was forged by a decade of hands-on problem-solving as an ASE Certified Mechanic. This experience taught me to tackle challenges head-on. As an avid learner and autodidact, I maintained a 3.88 GPA in my computer science studies at SNHU. However, my true passion lies in the practical application of code so I left to pursue a more hands-on learning approach. I am driven by the same curiosity that led me to take things apart as a child and now, I have the skills to build them back better. My experiences, including advocating for my child with autism, have taught me invaluable lessons in patience and purpose, which I bring to every project I work on.

Objective

My objective is to build things I enjoy and find purposeful. I also want to empower small businesses in my area by providing accessible, cutting-edge technological solutions on both the web and mobile devices. I am passionate about leveling the playing field, allowing smaller companies to compete with established enterprises by developing responsive web applications, creating intuitive mobile experiences, and integrating AI to streamline their systems. I bring a unique blend of problem-solving, relentless drive, and technical skill to help businesses grow and thrive in the modern digital landscape.
